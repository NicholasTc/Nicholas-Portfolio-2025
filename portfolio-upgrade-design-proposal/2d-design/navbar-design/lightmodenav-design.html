Design Improvements

Issue 1: N Logo & Toggle Colors

Before: Dark blue-purple gradient (#2563eb → #7c3aed) that was too dark and complex for light mode.

After: Balanced gradient using medium-toned blues (#60a5fa → #a78bfa) that shows up well on light backgrounds without being too dark or contrasting.

N

Issue 2: Scrolled Navbar Darkness

Before: Background of rgba(250, 251, 252, 0.85) created too much contrast and made content hard to see when scrolled.

After: Lighter background of rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.95) with subtle shadow for better visibility and reduced contrast. Content remains clearly visible.

N
Navigation content is clearly visible with improved contrast

Issue 3: Download Resume Button

Before: Full gradient background button that was too contrasting and heavy in light mode.

After: Outline style button with subtle border and hover state. Less visually heavy while maintaining clear call-to-action visibility.
